About Me

Meet Your Attorney

Anna Dichter

Attorney & Founder

Anna Dichter served as a Trial Attorney and Senior Litigation Counsel in the Office of Immigration Litigation at the U.S. Department of Justice Civil Division, where she managed all phases of federal litigation—from case assessment and strategy development through motion practice, discovery, depositions, court proceedings, and trial preparation.

She has practiced in over 20 federal district courts and 6 appellate courts across the United States, handling cases involving constitutional challenges, statutory interpretation, regulatory compliance, administrative law, and immigration law. Her DOJ experience includes class action litigation, e-discovery, and supporting U.S. Attorney's Offices nationwide.

Anna received the 2020 Civil Division Special Commendation Award for outstanding service on the COVID-19 litigation response team, and the 2021 Rookie of the Year Award for exceptional performance. She co-founded Lives in Translation at Rutgers, a program connecting bilingual students with immigrants and community organizations to provide interpreting and translation services.
